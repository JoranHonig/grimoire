
A flow which occurs whenever a new finding is discovered.

* [[proof of concept]] - a skill to automatically build a proof of concept (optional)
* [[finding]] - a skill to write, edit, improve findings
* [[scribe]] - will review finding for learnings
	* [[gnome]] - a gnome might be used to build a sigil
	* [[sigil]] - the sigil would immediately be ran (see also [[autonomous discovery]])
